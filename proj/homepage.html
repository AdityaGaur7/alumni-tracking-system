ALUMNI TRACKING SYSTEM

Website to eradicate the gap between Students and Alumni and make contacting the Alumni tractable and accessible to all hence improving the legacy of the institution. The web portal for our alumni tracking system enables colleges and universities to keep track of their alumni and for alumni to keep their information up-to-date. Features include login/logout, registration, friend requests, an events gallery, and the ability to leave comments/suggestions. The system also provides colleges with a wealth of information about their alumni, including contact details, career paths, and other important information. This information can be used to support alumni engagement, fundraising efforts, and other initiatives
